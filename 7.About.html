ABOUT US



Nestled in the heart of Aligarh, the Hotel The Royal Residency stands as a testament to unparalleled luxury and unmatched service. A mere 5 kilometers away from the Railway Station, our boutique hotel offers both accessibility and seclusion, providing a tranquil oasis amidst the bustling cityscape. Our offering of 16 meticulously designed rooms aims to cater to the needs of every guest. Modern amenities paired with our staff’s unwavering commitment to service ensure that every stay is comfortable and hassle-free. Come, be our guest at Hotel Royal Residency, Aligarh, and allow us to redefine luxury for you. The very moniker “AROMA” suggests a burst of flavor. With seating for 75, our qualified chef and dedicated team ensure that every guest is treated with unparalleled attention and respect, savoring every bite of our exquisite dishes. Experience exceptional service and culinary delights at our restaurant.
